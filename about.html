Hi, I’m Whitney!

I’m a freelance business consultant and registered teacher from Trinidad and Tobago. I have a background in Psychology and Human Resource Management, and my mission is to help businesses enhance their processes and empower learners to achieve their goals.

"12+ years in banking and finance, I am skilled in business operations, systems, and customer relationship management."

My personal journey through teaching and tutoring, allowed me to combine my passion for education with my professional skills. I’ve also worked in the non-profit sector, where I further developed my dedication to community support.

"Motherhood, Faith & Mental Health"

As a mother of one, I understand the need for balance and aim to create efficient solutions to streamline daily tasks. I'm a problem-solver who thrives on innovation, and I am motivated to continually learn and share my knowledge with others. My interest in human behaviour helps me to improve my practices and methods, and I am able to create effective strategies tailored to individual needs.

My faith in Christ has brought me here, and I feel led to build a community, and to advocate for lifelong learning. I am also dedicated to supporting mental wellness, and fostering growth in business and education.
